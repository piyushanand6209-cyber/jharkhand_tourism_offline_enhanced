Hundru Falls

Hundru Falls is formed by the Subarnarekha River and is one of the tallest and most-visited waterfalls near Ranchi (approx 320 feet / 98 m drop). Visitors should note seasonal hazards during the monsoon.

Quick facts
Height: Approx. 98 m (320 ft)
Nearest city: Ranchi (approx 45 km)
Best time: Octoberâ€“February (post-monsoon)

Overview

Hundru Falls is created where the Subarnarekha River drops from the Ranchi plateau, creating a dramatic cascade. It's popular for day trips from Ranchi but can be dangerous during heavy rains.

Visitor guidance

  • Follow local safety signage; avoid edges and slippery rocks, especially during/after monsoon.
  • Basic facilities are limited; plan accordingly.
  • Respect wildlife and local vendors; carry water and waste bags.

How to reach

About 45 km from Ranchi by road; taxis and buses available.

Suggested day plan

  1. Leave early from Ranchi, reach the viewpoint, enjoy short walks and photography, return by afternoon.
