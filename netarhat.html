Netarhat is a hill station and plateau famous for dramatic sunrises, pine-clad ridges, and quiet nature trails. Known spots include Magnolia Point, Koel View Point and Upper/Lower Ghaghri Falls.

Overview

Netarhat, often called the "Queen of Chotanagpur", is a quiet plateau offering sunrise views and oak-pine forests. It was developed during the British era and contains several viewpoints and waterfalls within short drives.

Things to do

  • Sunrise at Magnolia Point or Sunrise Point
  • Short treks to Upper and Lower Ghaghri Falls
  • Birdwatching and quiet nature walks
  • Visit local tribal villages respectfully to learn about crafts

Practical tips

  • Road access is the most common; hire local transport from Latehar/Ranchi.
  • Carry warm clothes for mornings and avoid littering â€” respect local commons.
  • Limited medical facilities â€” carry basic meds.

How to reach

By road from Ranchi/Latehar. Latehar is the nearest railhead; Ranchi airport is the nearest airport. Local buses connect to Netarhat, but private vehicles give flexibility.

Suggested 2-day itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrive, sunset at Magnolia Point.
  2. Day 2: Sunrise at Sunrise Point, short trek to Lower Ghaghri, local village visit.
