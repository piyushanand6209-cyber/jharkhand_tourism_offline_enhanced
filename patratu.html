Patratu Valley & Dam

Patratu offers a scenic valley, reservoir views and a winding drive; the dam was built to supply water and is a popular picnic and sunset spot.

Quick facts
Best time: Year-round (pleasant in winter)
Nearest city: Ramgarh / Ranchi (35â€“60 km)

Overview

Patratu Dam stores water from local rivers and has panoramic viewpoints. The zig-zag road is popular for drives and photography. Infrastructure at times varies; check local notices for services.

Visitor tips

How to reach

Accessible from Ranchi, Ramgarh and Hazaribagh by road. Patratu town is nearby with local connections.
