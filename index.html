Top Experiences

Community Homestays

Stay with host families to learn local crafts and cuisine. Many villages host homestays â€” always book in advance and follow house rules.

Wildlife & Birdwatching

Betla and nearby sanctuaries are rich in birdlife. Carry binoculars and learn to identify resident species with local guides.

Waterfall Trails

Explore post-monsoon cascades like Hundru and Dugda. Follow safety advice and avoid risky edges.

Festivals & Culture

Plan trips during Sarhul (spring) or Karam (harvest) to witness tribal dances and fairs. Respect rituals and ask permission before photographing.
