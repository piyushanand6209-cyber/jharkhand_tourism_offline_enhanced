Betla National Park (Palamau Tiger Reserve)

Betla is part of the Palamau Tiger Reserve complex and offers safaris, rich birdlife, and community eco-trails. It is one of the earliest protected areas in the region with guided experiences.

Overview

Betla National Park forms part of the Palamau Tiger Reserve and supports diverse mammals, reptiles and birds. Community-run eco-trails connect visitors with local conservation efforts and livelihoods.

Things to do

  • Early morning jeep safari (book through forest department/local operators)
  • Birdwatching and guided nature walks
  • Visit tribal villages to learn about sustainable livelihoods

How to reach

Nearest railhead: Daltonganj (approx 25 km). Road links from Ranchi and Daltonganj.
